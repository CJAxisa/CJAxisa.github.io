Discus Battle Gladiators

About

Discus Battle Gladiators is an abstract strategy board game. Each player has 8 pieces that they use to move a neutral Discus piece to the end of the board. There are 3 different types of pieces; Interceptors, Cyborgs, and Brute. Each of these have different abilities. Our inspiration for this game was trying to combine Chess and Ultimate Frisbee.For more information on the game, please watch the video to the left.

Development

Discus Battle gladiators was developed by a team of 5 over an 11 week period for a class. I helped with game design and balance. Working on this project was very satisfying, as my team was very skilled and well rounded. We had a variety of design ideas which helped us create a game that was easy to learn but had a high skill ceiling. After a bit of research, we found a game similar to our concept, Ultimate. After this we made our game more complex strategically and more gritty tonally to differentiate us. Overall, I think this was a good move for our project. This led to us introducing the kill mechanic for the brute class. This was one of the most important decisions in the game's development. It fundamentally changed the game from the prototype we had before, and it solved a lot of gameplay problems and balancing issues we were having.
